  • Balance is not when all the things get equal time each day, but when you concentrate on one thing at a time for a few days and then switch to another for a few days. Instead of thinking about it as a list of things you don't have time for, pick whichever one interests you the most right now and do it for 30 minutes. Do the same thing night after night, until a different hobby has more of a spark. Hobbies are for enrichment!
